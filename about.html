Our Mission

Our mission is to ignite a passion for technology in young minds by teaching them the essential skills of computer science and programming. We aim to provide a solid foundation in Python programming, leading them towards a deeper understanding of AI and deep learning.

Program Overview

Python Programming

Our Python programming courses are designed for kids, starting with the basics and gradually moving to more advanced concepts. Kids will learn how to write short programs, solve problems, and think logically.

Introduction to AI and Deep Learning

Once they are comfortable with Python, our students will be introduced to AI and deep learning. They will learn about neural networks, data processing, and how AI is used in the real world. By the end of the program, they will have a solid foundation to pursue further studies in these fields.

Our Expertise

At Kids AI Programming, we pride ourselves on having a team of world-class experts who are dedicated to preparing the next generation of AI leaders. Our tutors have extensive experience in both academia and industry, with deep expertise in AI, deep learning, and computer science education.

One of our experts has significant research experience in AI and deep learning, with published research papers in top-tier journals and conferences. They bring cutting-edge knowledge to the classroom, ensuring that our curriculum is aligned with the latest developments in the field.

Another one of our educators specializes in teaching computer science to children from grade 5 and up. With years of experience in making complex topics accessible to young learners, they focus on building strong foundational skills that will set our students up for future success in any field they choose.
